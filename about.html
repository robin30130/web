About Us

The Aspic Rock is a rock band originary of France. With their electrifying performances and industrial music, we have captured the hearts of millions of fans around the world.

Formed by lead vocalist Dexter, guitarist Robin, bassist Tommy Thunder, and drummer Rick Roll, The Aspic Rock quickly rose to fame with our first album "Under the Laws" which topped the charts for 2023 year.

Over the years, the band has toured extensively, headlining major festivals and sharing the stage with iconic rock bands such as Metallica, Iron Maiden, The Offspring. our energetic live shows are known for our explosive energy and passionate performances.

With our two albums, The Aspic's continues to inspire and influence generations of rock music lovers. we are the legacy of the greatest rock bands of the 90's.
